About Us

Welcome to Color Tech, the perfect blend of color and
technology. We are a leading supplier of liquid colourants and
dispersions for the paints and coatings industry with more than
25 years’ experience within the coatings industry. Our innovative
and tested solutions provide value, reliability, and versatility to
manufacturers for a range of paints and coatings applications.

At ColorTech, we take pride in delivering the best products to
our customers. We understand their unique requirements, and
our diverse range of cutting-edge offerings ensures that we have
the perfect solution for every need. Our primary focus is
delivering unparalleled service and groundbreaking colorant
technology, ensuring a personalized approach for each of our
esteemed clients.

As an independent local manufacturer, we take a hands-on
approach to support our customers. We work closely with them
to create tailored solutions that align with the dynamic nature
of the paints and coatings market. Our dedicated Quality Control
team works directly with our customers’ color labs,
guaranteeing consistent color cross all our products.
At ColorTech, we prioritize continuous research and
development. This dedication enables us to offer a
comprehensive range of innovative products and solutions that
bring substantial value to our clients’ operations. We strive to
stay at the forefront of the industry, consistently introducing
cutting-edge advancements to meet the evolving needs of our
customers.

ColorTech provides an extensive selection of solvent-based,
water-based, and universal colorants suitable for both interior
and exterior paints and coatings applications. With our
comprehensive range, you have the freedom to explore endless
possibilities and bring vibrant colors to life.
Our commitment to excellence, customer centric approach, and
unwavering pursuit of innovation set us apart as a trusted
partner in the colorant industry. With ColorTech Technologies,
you can expect nothing less than exceptional products and
unwavering support for your success.
Choose ColorTech, and together, we can unlock a world of
limitless color possibilities.
