Our Products

PVA

Polyvinyl Acetate is a water-based paint which are mainly used for interior walls and ceilings. PVA has very good properties and is mainly used for interior walls and ceilings, a primer for drywall, for a low-sheen finishes. It isgreat to be used as an cost effective option and suitable for non high traffic areas.

QD

Quick Dry paint is typically water-based or solvent based and is formulated to dry significantly faster tahn any other standard paint. QD is suitable for interior, exterior, metal and wood surfaces. Where mainly used for touch-ups and small project and high traffic areas.

Enamel

Enamel paint dries hard, durable and has a glossy finish. It has great properties and works well when used on metal surfaces, any wood work, appliances, bathroom and kitchen walls and floor.

Epoxy

Epoxy flooring is a system where multiple layers of epoxy (thermosetting resin) is applied to a concrete surface. Epoxy flooring is fit for all facilities from industrial spaces to basement flooring.

Automotive

Automotive paint is formulated for vehicles which include, cars, trucks, motocycles, heavy machinery and tractors. It provides protection against enviromental factors and rough terrain.

Tapes

Adhesive tapes are designed for heavy-duty applications in industrial and commercial settings, they come in a wide variety and provide strong adhesion, durability and resistance.

Wood Finish

Wood Finish refers to a wide catagory of products. These products are applied to wood surfaces to protect them and enhances their appearance. The Wood Finishes ranges from clear to those that highlight the natural beauty. The Finish can be used on furnature, flooring, doors, trims and woodens structures and art.

Road Marking

Road Marking paint is used for marking roadways, parking lots, airports runways, and all paved surfaces.This paint is a specialized formula designed for paved surface, it's durable, visible and is resistant chemicals, weather and wear.

Soaps

Soaps come in different types and formulas, which each have a spesific purpose. We provided a wide varity of soaps at Color Tech.

Tinter Bottles

Tinter bottles contain concentrated pigments that can be mixed into base paints to achieve a wide color range. These pigments are crucial for tinting and color matching for paints.
